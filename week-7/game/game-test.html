Precambrian Era

It'll be a catastrophe, they said. It's too unstable, they said. Well, vindication is sweet. Almost as sweet as my new ride. That's right, the timecycle. It's like nothing else. Feeling the winds of time in your face... speeding through a dimension no human has ever breached...

Oh, well, I can't reminisce all day. The lava's getting pretty close, and the air quality isn't great here in the Precambrian Era. And this bike's not going to push itself.

Actually, it's better that the fuel compartment blew up the way it did. Now the fuel crystals are scattered across the eons like breadcrumbs, so if I follow them I should just be able to keep the motor running all the way home. Looks like there are three crystals here. Just enough to get me to the next era before I get fried.

Move ⚘ with the arrow buttons to collect the fuel crystals (⬢) to escape the Precambrian Era, and start your journey home. But don't let the lava touch you!
