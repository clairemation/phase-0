Timecycle

Precambrian Era

It will only lead to catastrophe, my colleagues pled. Good folks, but so short-sighted. I know what I'm doing--I'm Dr. Allie Ampersand (⚘), the world's leading chronolocomotive systems engineer (not that being the only one makes that any less significant). And my timecycle, complete at last, is a work of beauty and genius... and, oh... the sweetest of rides. Feeling the winds of time in your face... accomplishing what no human ever has before...

Oh, well, I can't reminisce all day. The lava's getting pretty close, and the air quality isn't great here in the Precambrian Era. And this bike's not going to push itself. You're stranded in the Precambrian Era, your timecycle's fuel chamber is busted, and the trilithium crystals it uses for fuel are scattered across the eons.

Move with the arrow buttons to collect the trilithium crystals (⬢) in this era to escape to the next one, and start your journey home. But don't let the lava touch you!
